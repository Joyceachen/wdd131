Uganda, Kampala
Official Flag of Uganda

Uganda is a landlocked country in East Africa, known for its diverse culture, rich history, and breathtaking natural beauty. Bordered by Kenya, Tanzania, Rwanda,the Democratic Republic of the Congo, and South Sudan, Uganda's economy is driven by agriculture, with main crops including coffee, cotton, and maize. The country is home to numerous ethnic groups, including the Baganda, Ankole, and Basoga, and has a growing telecommunications and aviation sector. Kampala is the capital and largest city, serving as the country's economic, cultural, and political hub. Uganda faces challenges related to human rights, environmental concerns, and oil development projects, but its natural resources, including oil and gas reserves, offer opportunities for growth and development.
